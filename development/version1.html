we are the 4%

by nicole laguerta

(scroll to continue)

93% of foster youth aspire to attend college, yet only 4% graduate with a bachelor's degree by age 26, compared to 50% of their non-foster peers. Those from foster care who do attend college face numerous challenges, including financial constraints, lack of family support, academic underpreparedness, mental health struggles, and the absence of emotional support from caring adults.
