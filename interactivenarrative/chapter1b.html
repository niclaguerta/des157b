Stay Home: Neutral Ending

Ke$ha and CJ, united in their fears, contemplate the potential hazards that may be concealed within the dense foliage of the forest. They weigh the promise of the unknown against the impending risks—the shadowy depths of the Enchanted Forest could be a haven for menacing creatures and hidden pitfalls. The thought of venturing into such perilous territory fills them with trepidation.

After much deliberation, they decide to disregard the tantalizing call of the map. Choosing safety over the thrilling uncertainty, they retreat to the familiarity of their cozy homes. They return to their routine lives, indulging in the joys of feline leisure and the simplicity of their peaceful existence.

Yet, despite their decision, the allure of the map and its promise of a thrilling adventure persist. The image of the ancient treasure, the thrill of the chase, and the enchanting whispers of the Enchanted Forest never truly leave their minds. An undertow of wonder pulls at them, sparking their imaginations and leaving them with a lingering sense of curiosity.

Every rustle of a leaf, every whisper of the wind seems to echo the forest's call. Every serene sunset and peaceful dawn is clouded with dreams of the adventure they declined. They can't help but ponder—what marvels did the map hide? What ancient secrets lay in the heart of the forest? The taste of the untaken path, of an adventure forsaken, leaves them with a subtle, nagging sense of what could have been—a tale of adventure and discovery forever untold.
