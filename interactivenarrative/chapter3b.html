Demand Assistance

Stepping forward with an uncharacteristic air of arrogance, Ke$ha and CJ address the Council of Elders. Their usually respectful demeanors are replaced with a brash and demanding stance. Their words, devoid of the humility and reverence usually shown to the Council, echo through the ancient temple. They demand assistance, their pride overshadowing the urgency and gravity of their predicament.

Their audacious behavior strikes a discordant note within the hallowed chambers of the Council. The esteemed elders, Meatball the High King, Casey the Scholar, and Kitty Boy the Chancellor, exchange surprised and disapproving glances. This was not the respectful approach they expected, nor the conduct becoming of true heroes.

Offended by Ke$ha and CJ's arrogance, the Council starts to question their motives. Could they be driven by personal glory rather than the welfare of Meowstoria? Are they truly ready to face the wicked witch, or are they blinded by their newfound fame and the power of the amulet?

The Council, in their wisdom, decides that such brashness cannot be rewarded. They refuse to provide the much-needed assistance and guidance, reminding Ke$ha and CJ that courage without humility and respect is nothing more than recklessness.

Left to their own devices, Ke$ha and CJ are now severely underprepared to face the powerful witch. Without the wisdom and strategic insights of the Council, without the enchanted items they could have provided, they stand woefully ill-equipped to confront the grave threat looming over Meowstoria.
