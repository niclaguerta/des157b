Chapter 2: The Wicked Witch

As their lives began to return to a semblance of normalcy, an unexpected turn of events took place. Bandit and Kobe, two of the friends they had made during their earlier exploits in the Enchanted Forest, came to visit. Their faces, usually filled with mirth and mischief, bore a somber expression that immediately caught Ke$ha and CJ's attention.

Bandit and Kobe came bearing grim newsâ€”a wicked witch, intrigued by tales of their newfound fame and the magical amulet they possessed, had set her sights on them. She thrived on the sickness and blight of the land, a power Ke$ha and CJ had unwittingly stripped from her with the acquisition of the amulet. The witch was a known scourge, thriving on the miseries of the land. Ke$ha and CJ's actions had disrupted her source of power, and she wanted the amulet to regain control and inflict more suffering upon Meowstoria.

Suddenly, Ke$ha and CJ found themselves at the heart of a critical decision. The witch was a formidable adversary. Could they, in their current state, dare to face her and protect the amulet? Or should they first approach the ancient Council of Elders for help, understanding that this decision might leave parts of the Enchanted Forest at the mercy of the witch's blight?
