About Me

Hello! Thanks for playing my game. My name is Nicole, and I'm a student who is currently a double major in Design and CDM at UC Davis. My world revolves around art, cats, books, and video games, which I find endlessly inspiring. Naturally, I decided to blend these passions for my final project.

Leveraging my skills in HTML/CSS, Adobe Creative Cloud, and AI, I brought Paws of Destiny to life as my final project for DES 117: Interactive Media. Every piece of content within the game has been exclusively designed and crafted by me.

Creativity is more than just a process; it's a passion. Each creation carries a part of me, and sharing them brings me immense joy. I hope you enjoyed playing Paws of Destiny as much as I loved creating it!
