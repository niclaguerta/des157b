Confront the Witch: Bad Ending

Guided by their collective courage and an unwavering sense of duty, Ke$ha and CJ make the fateful decision to face the wicked witch. They follow Bandit and Kobe, their hearts filled with a mixture of fear and resolve, but they know they must face this threat head-on to protect their land and their friends.

Their journey takes them deep into the heart of the Enchanted Forest, to the witch's lair, a place shrouded in a foreboding aura that chills them to their very bones. As they step into the den of darkness, their bravado meets the icy gaze of the witch, a chilling sight that sends a surge of adrenaline coursing through their veins.

The confrontation begins with a whirlwind of chaotic energy, as magic clashes with bravery. However, the battle takes a devastating turn. The witch, with her formidable dark magic, manages to ensnare Ke$ha and Kobe in a trap of shadowy tendrils. The sight of their friends, helpless and entrapped, strikes a blow more potent than any physical attack.

CJ and Bandit, though driven by the desperate need to help their friends, find themselves overpowered. They narrowly escape the witch's clutches, leaving their captured companions behind in a heartbreaking moment of helplessness.

Returning to the safety of their home, their hearts are heavy with guilt and sorrow. They are haunted by the sight of Ke$ha and Kobe, left at the mercy of the wicked witch. The precious amulet, now in the possession of the witch, seems a poignant symbol of their failure. As CJ and Bandit lick their wounds, they are tormented by the repercussions of their decision, with the echo of their friends' captures casting a long, dark shadow over their hearts.
