Seek Help

Upon assessing the perilous situation, Ke$ha and CJ come to a sobering realization. The wicked witch's growing influence and her desire for the magical amulet present a threat of an unprecedented scale. It dawned on them that despite their newfound power, this formidable challenge may be beyond their solitary capabilities.

After thoughtful deliberation, they decide to seek the wisdom of the Council of Elders. Known far and wide across Meowstoria for their age-old wisdom, the Council of Elders could guide them on how best to navigate this dire situation and counteract the witch's nefarious plans. They will embark on a journey to the Council, though it comes with a painful cost.

The journey to the Council of Elders, residing atop the tallest peak of Meowstoria, is not only treacherous but also time-consuming. This daunting voyage implies that while they undertake this journey, the Enchanted Forest would remain unprotected and susceptible to the wicked witch's influence. They would essentially be sacrificing a portion of the forest to buy themselves some time.

Yet, they know that this sacrifice, however painful, is a necessary one. The fate of their entire world hinges on their decision and their ability to counter the witch's menacing intentions. Strengthened by their resolve, they brace themselves for the hardships ahead, prepared to face whatever challenges the journey throws their way. Every step they take is a step towards safeguarding their homeland and preserving the harmony of Meowstoria.
